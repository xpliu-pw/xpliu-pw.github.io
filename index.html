Research 科学研究

Research Interests 研究方向

Lie algebras, quantum algebras and their representation theory, including: infinite-dimensional Lie algebras (e.g., Kac-Moody algebras), quantum groups, quantum affine algebras, Yangians, and related representation problems. 李代数、量子代数及其表示理论，包括：无穷维李代数（如 Kac-Moody 代数）、量子群、量子仿射代数、Yangian 以及相关表示问题.
